Sickle Cell Insights

What is Sickle Cell Disease?

Sickle Cell Disease is a genetic disorder affecting red blood cells, causing pain and health complications.

How can I support this initiative?

You can volunteer, donate, or spread awareness about Sickle Cell Disease in your community.

What are the symptoms of SCD?

Symptoms include pain crises, fatigue, and frequent infections. It's a tough battle!

Can SCD be cured?

Currently, there is no universal cure, but treatments can manage symptoms effectively.

How to prevent SCD in children?

Genetic counseling and screening can help prevent SCD in future generations.
